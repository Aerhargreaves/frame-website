ABOUT


I am Alice, a maker, designer and repairist. Though I think ‘frame artist’ might fit best?

I craft collections of handmade frames that tell stories about people, their communities and our environment.

Through each piece I try to explore different ideas, identities, locations and behaviours. Ask questions about how we value our resources and who our world is designed for.

I also design bespoke pieces & refurbish antique frames, listening to your stories and crafting objects that celebrate your unique features.
