ABOUT

I believe every happy creative has that one object that just fills them with excitement. For me that is spectacles.

I am Alice, a frame artist. Working in the medium of spectacle making.

Through limited collections of handmade frames I try to explore different ideas, identities, locations and behaviours. Ask questions about how we value our resources and who our world is designed for.

I also design bespoke pieces, I try to explore the stories of others, celebrate their unique features and amplify their personality...
