BESPOKE


Working with bespoke clients is one of my favourite ways to bring a new frame to life. If you've struggled to get that perfect fit in the past or perhaps have a story
you'd like to tell, this is an opportunity to do it. Throughout a 6 month period we will work togther to create a completely unique frame, tailored to you, handmade by me.

Starting at Â£800 - The process involves 3 in-person meetings. The first to take your measurements and discuss what you'd like to achieve, the second to present design options and pick which one you'd like to take forward. Finally, we check the fit with a 3D-printed prototype and select your final materials & components.

Then it's time to get in the workshop!
Each frame is hand-cut, shaped and polished - meaning it is impossible to replicate, a true one-off.
Your frame will always be fitted with traditional pinned hinges, that way it can be repaired for years to come.

The aim, to create a future heirloom.
